Join Our Team

Become a RoboPhantom

Are you passionate about robotics, programming, engineering, or simply want to be part of an amazing team? We're always looking for dedicated individuals who share our enthusiasm for innovation and competition.

As a member of RoboPhantoms, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Design and build competition robots
  • Develop your programming and engineering skills
  • Compete in FIRST Tech Challenge events
  • Participate in community outreach activities
  • Work with a supportive team of mentors and peers
  • Build lasting friendships and professional connections

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Fill out our online application form below. Tell us about yourself, your interests, and why you want to join RoboPhantoms.

2

Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview with our team leaders and coaches. This helps us get to know you better.

3

Trial Period

New members participate in a trial period where you'll work on projects and learn about our team culture and processes.

4

Official Membership

Upon successful completion of the trial period, you'll become an official RoboPhantom team member!
