Our Outreach

Spreading awareness and inspiring the next generation

One of our biggest missions is to spread awareness and recognition for FIRST and inspire young kids to get involved in the robotics/STEM field at an early age! When conducting outreaches, we want to make sure we have a variety, and not just one type of outreach. We do this by splitting our events into 4 different categories: STEM, Non-STEM, Political, and Global.

STEM Outreach

Fort Bend Library

Fort Bend Library

Went to the Fort Bend Library to show our robot to kids and spark their enthusiasm for robotics. We also let them drive the robot to get them a feel of how FTC works.

Telica

Telica

Set up a booth at Telica - a local cultural event - to explain what FIRST is and how they can be involved in what FIRST has to offer. We also demonstrated our robot and let kids that were curious to drive the robot.

CCFFN

CCFFN

Set up a booth at CCFFN - a local community carnival - to engage kids and inspire them into STEM using our robot and showing them how unlocking your imaginations can lead to amazing products!

Summer Workshop 2025

Our 2025 Summer Workshop

We hosted a 4-hour intensive workshop geared to grades 3-6. In the workshop, we led kids through the FTC experience and taught fun and engaging robotics lessons about building, CAD, programming, and more! The workshop included multiple activities to offer a fun and interactive way of learning.

Party At the Plaza

Party At the Plaza

Hosted a booth at the "Party at the Plaza" event - a city operated event - where we introduced families to the mission of FIRST and shared ways they could get involved. We also had the opportunity to demonstrate our robot and speak with the Mayor of Sugar Land.

Non-STEM Outreach

Book Drive

Book Drive

We conducted our very own book drive! We partnered up with local non-profit Books Between Kids to conduct a book drive to donate books to the less fortunate. We collected more than 150 books from FTC teams, friends, family, and strangers.

Gigi's Playhouse

Gigi's Playhouse

Went to Gigi's Playhouse - a down syndrome playhouse - to demonstrate FIRST values and inspire the kids with our robot.

Political Outreach

Judge Sonia Rash

Judge Sonia Rash

Met with Fort Bend Judge Sonia Rash to ask about spreading awareness about FIRST and robotics.

Tony Flores

Tony Flores

Set up a meeting with Tony Flores (Chief of staff for Congressman Dr. Lalani) to advocate for more awareness to FIRST and possible government funding for all community FTC teams.

Madison Yandell

Madison Yandell

Presented to the State Budget Advisor, Madison Yandell to advocate on behalf of FTC community teams to receive some sort of government or central funding.

Global Outreach

Desire Orphanage

Desire Orphanage

Visited HIV+ boys in an orphanage in India and inspired them to get involved with FIRST robotics.

FTC Team Collaboration

FTC Team Meetings

FTC Team Meetings

Set up meetings with multiple FTC teams to discuss how we would tackle the technical and social aspects of this year's challenge. Shared ideas on how we operate as teams.
